1. Membership & Eligibility
  • Any individual above 18 years of age may register and request to join a chit group.
  • Membership is subject to admin approval and available capacity in the chit group.
  • Once the chit's start date has passed, no new members may join that group.
  • Each member must provide accurate personal details (name, email, phone, address).
  • Members are responsible for keeping their login credentials secure.
  • The admin reserves the right to reject or revoke membership for valid reasons.
2. Payment Obligations
  • Members must pay the fixed monthly installment on or before the due date each month.
  • Late payment fine: ₹20 per day from the day after the due date until payment is made.
  • Payment must be made via the QR code provided by the admin.
  • Members must upload a clear screenshot as proof of payment.
  • Payment is considered confirmed only after admin verification and approval.
  • Rejected payments must be re-submitted with correct proof.
  • Outstanding dues and fines will be deducted from any settlement amount.
3. Auction / Bidding Rules
  • One auction is conducted per month for each active chit group.
  • All eligible active members will receive an email announcement before the auction.
  • Members who have already won an auction in that chit are NOT eligible to bid again.
  • The member who places the lowest bid wins the lump sum payout for that month.
  • Winning bid amount must be within the min/max range set by admin for that chit.
  • Payout to the winner = Winning Bid Amount − Admin Commission.
  • The admin commission percentage is pre-configured and displayed on the chit.
  • Payout is released only after explicit admin approval.
4. Early Exit / Settlement
  • A member may request early exit from a chit before its completion date.
  • A 2% deduction is applied on the total amount paid by the member.
  • All pending dues, overdue fines, and outstanding amounts will be deducted before settlement.
  • Settlement requires admin approval. The admin reserves the right to approve or reject.
  • Settlement confirmation will be sent to the member's registered email.
  • Once exited, the member cannot re-join the same chit group.
5. Chit Completion & Profit Distribution
  • Members who complete the full duration of the chit are eligible for profit/dividend share.
  • Profit share is calculated based on the chit's configuration and admin discretion.
  • A final maturity statement and payment history summary will be provided upon completion.
  • The final settlement amount will be communicated via email and dashboard.

  • A PDF digital certificate is automatically generated and maintained in the system.
  • A copy is accessible to the member and admin through the platform.
