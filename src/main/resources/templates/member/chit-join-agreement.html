CHIT FUND PARTICIPATION AGREEMENT

This agreement is entered into between YGC Internal (the "Company") and the subscriber (the "Member") for participation in the chit fund scheme "Chit Name".

1. CONTRIBUTIONS

The Member agrees to pay a monthly contribution of ₹ on or before the due date each month for a period of months. A late payment fine of ₹20 per day will be levied for delayed payments.

2. AUCTION & BIDDING

Monthly auctions are conducted among all active members. The member who bids the lowest amount wins the prize for that month. Once a member wins an auction, they cannot bid again in the same chit. The Company will deduct a commission of % from the winning bid amount as administrative fees.

3. BID CALCULATION & PAYOUT

The payout to the winner is calculated as: Winner's Bid Amount – Company Commission. The remaining members continue to pay their monthly contributions until chit completion.

4. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

This chit fund is operated in compliance with the Chit Funds Act, 1982 and applicable RBI guidelines. Minimum bid is not less than 70% of the total chit value. All transactions are audited.

The Member authorises YGC Internal to collect and process their personal information (name, contact details, payment history) solely for the purpose of chit fund operations. Data will not be shared with third parties without consent.

Early exit from the chit is permitted only under exceptional circumstances and subject to approval by the administrator. Forfeiture or penalty may apply as per the Company's policy.
