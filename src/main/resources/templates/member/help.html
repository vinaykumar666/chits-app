How It Works — App Flow
  1. Register — Create your account. A temporary password is sent to your email.
  2. Change Password — On first login, set your own secure password.
  3. Browse Chits — Find available chit groups and request to join.
  4. Admin Approval — Admin reviews and approves your membership.
  5. Accept Terms — Accept Terms & Conditions. Your digital certificate is auto-generated.
  6. Pay Monthly — Pay via admin QR code and upload screenshot proof.
  7. Admin Verifies — Admin approves/rejects your payment.
  8. Monthly Auction — Participate in bid auctions. Lowest bidder wins the lump sum.
  9. Chit Completion — Receive profit share and final settlement statement.
Payment Rules
  • Pay your monthly installment on or before the due date.
  • Late fine: ₹20 per day after the due date. Avoid delays!
  • Upload a clear screenshot as payment proof.
  • Payment status: Pending → Approved or Rejected
  • Overdue payments affect your auction eligibility.
Auction / Bidding Rules
  • Auction happens once every month for the chit.
  • All active members receive email announcement.
  • Lowest bidder wins the lump sum payout.
  • Each member can win the auction only once per chit.
  • Payout = Winning Bid − Admin Commission.
  • Bid range is set by admin during chit creation.
Early Exit Rules
Early exit is discouraged. Please complete your chit for maximum benefit.
  • 2% deduction on total amount paid.
  • All pending dues & late fines will be deducted first.
  • Settlement requires admin approval.
  • Settlement confirmation sent to your email.
Completion Benefits
  • Receive your profit/dividend share.
  • Get a detailed maturity statement.
  • Complete payment history summary provided.
  • Digital certificate auto-generated on joining.
Frequently Asked Questions

A chit fund is a savings scheme where a group of people contribute a fixed amount every month. Each month, one member receives the pooled amount through a bid/auction. It's a traditional Indian savings & credit instrument.

The monthly amount is set by the admin when creating the chit. You can see it on the chit details page before joining.

A late fine of ₹20 per day will be charged from the due date. Your payment status will show as OVERDUE. Please contact admin at +91 8919508889 if you have any issues.

Your digital membership certificate is automatically generated once you accept the Terms & Conditions on your membership details page.
